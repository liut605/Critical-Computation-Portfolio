Lost Weaver: Thread by Thread

Step into the delicate world of a spider—a creature burdened by a missing instinct. As the player, you guide this lost weaver, teaching a skill all spiders are destined to master. No keyboard, no mouse—just your hands captured by your webcam. Spider's survival hangs by a single thread. Will the dance of your hands weave the threads of hope?

Experimental Clock

Drawing inspiration from a recent hike to Harriman State Park at the beggining of autumn, I saw the change of seaons in the gradients of leaves. What if I could control or reserve time or season? I created a wheel where you can drag the mouse to control the gradient. Clockwise movement = green to red; anti-clockwise = back to green. Random radius of the circle is also generated every 30 seconds to create visual variation.

Drag mouse to control rotation; press any key to restart.

Optical Illusion

I have been increasingly interested in eye tracking technologies and was inspired by the screen protection/ encrypted webpage examples mentioned in CC lecture. I decided to create an interaction where the encrypted pattern of an eye reveals itself when a user gaze at the screen. If you are having trouble callibrating the facial recognition / eye tracking, here is the link to mock up where the interaction is triggered when mouse is pressed.

Click while looking at the cursor to begin callibration.
